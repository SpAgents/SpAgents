## Projects, Responsibilities, and Alignment with Employer Goals

During my internship, I worked on a comprehensive **axSpA (Axial Spondyloarthritis) Intelligent Diagnostic System**, which represents the company's flagship healthcare AI product. This project directly aligned with Vixtel's mission to develop practical, AI-driven diagnostic tools for clinical use.

My key responsibilities included:

1. **Big Data Infrastructure & Preprocessing**: I designed and implemented scalable ETL pipelines for processing large-scale heterogeneous medical datasets, including DICOM images and structured EHR data. This involved developing standardized preprocessing protocols for high-dimensional MRI volumes (24×512×512) using percentile-based linear scaling and shape-preserving interpolation.

2. **Multimodal AI Integration**: I integrated a hybrid diagnostic system combining multi-agent large language models (DeepSeek LLM) with custom deep learning components. This included developing the **EdemaSystem module** for automated lesion detection and SPARCC scoring, featuring two-stage segmentation and region-level edema classification.

3. **Model Evaluation & Statistical Testing**: I developed a robust evaluation pipeline using bootstrap methods and McNemar tests to calculate confidence intervals and significance values for key diagnostic metrics (accuracy, sensitivity, specificity).

4. **Documentation & Research**: I contributed to internal documentation and technical design reports, and participated in drafting a white paper on "Large-Scale Multimodal Diagnostic Architectures in Rheumatology."

These responsibilities directly supported the company's goal of creating clinically viable AI diagnostic tools while maintaining high standards of scientific rigor and statistical validation.
